# Privacy Policy – AttendCrew

Effective Date: 1st April, 2026

AttendCrew ("we", "our", or "us") operates a workforce attendance management application designed for internal company use.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Personal Information

* Employee name and basic profile details

### b) Location Data

* We collect location data to verify that employees are present at authorized work sites during check-in and check-out.

### c) Camera and Face Data

* The app uses the device camera to capture facial data for identity verification.
* Facial data is converted into embeddings and used only for authentication purposes.

### d) Device Information

* Basic device information may be collected for security and logging purposes.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for:

* Employee attendance tracking
* Identity verification (face recognition)
* Location validation for attendance
* Improving system security and preventing misuse

---

## 3. Data Sharing

* We do NOT sell or share personal data with third parties.
* Data is only accessible to authorized company administrators.

---

## 4. Data Security

* We implement appropriate security measures to protect user data.
* Facial data is stored securely and used only for verification purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

* Data is retained only as long as required for operational and legal purposes.

---

## 6. User Rights

Users can contact their organization administrator to:

* Access their data
* Request correction or deletion

---

## 7. Permissions Used

* Camera: Required for face verification
* Location: Required for attendance validation

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes.
